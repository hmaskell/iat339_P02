Branding

What does the company do, or offer?

RDL is a recreational adult dodgeball league. We play 10 weeks of dodgeball, with one day of playoffs.


Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

RDL would be a fun way to meet new people through some sort of physical activity. Similarly to many other recreational leagues, there is a focus on community. It is a great league to get involved with Dodgeball in as it is not as competitive as its older brother, VDL.


Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

It may not seem like it, but dodgeball is a real workout. Just like any sport, there is so much to learn, and everyone's gotta start somewhere. That being said, you might as well start in a league where you can meet cool people, and hone your skills until your wings fly, where you grow to be the strongest dodgeball player there ever was.

Persona

Ellen (21, Female) just moved to Richmond this year for school and she is looking for a way to keep fit and get involved in her community. Since she doesn't know many people, she wants to find a way to make new friends without taking up too much of her study time. She comes across an RDL flyer at her university and decides to sign up as a single player after coming to a drop in night. She is placed on a team and plays with them every week.
