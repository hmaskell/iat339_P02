RDL Dodgeball

Richmond Dodgeball League (RDL) is a non-profit adult recreational league. RDL is part of the International Dodgeball Association (IDA) and follows their rules and code of conduct. Teams of 6-12 players (at least 2 of which must identify as female) play against 4 or 5 teams per night.
