Style Guide for Richmond Dodgeball League

Colour Palette

Orange Swatch

Default State: #F15F23; Active, Focus, and Hover States: #EF7362;

Blue Swatch

Default State: #008FD3; Active, Focus, and Hover States: #43ADE0;

Black Swatch

Default State: #212121; Active, Focus, and Hover States: #4C4C4C;

White Swatch

Default State: #FFFFFF; Active, Focus, and Hover States: #E8E8E8;

Fonts

Headings and Title text

font-bold: Quicksand Bold, sans-serif;

h1, h2, h3, h4 {
 font-family: 'Quicksand', Helvetica, sans-serif;
}

Body Text

font: Quicksand, sans-serif;

body, p, ol, ul {
 font-family: 'Quicksand', Helvetica, sans-serif;
}

Caption Text

font: Quicksand Italicized, sans-serif;

figcaption {
 font-family: 'Quicksand', Helvetica, sans-serif;
}

CSS selector:

Sample code:

 <button class="button">Reset</button>
</form>

Paragraphs

CSS selector:

p

Sample code:

<p>
 The Richmond Dodgeball League was founded in the Fall of 2010 as an extension of the Vancouver Dodgeball League. What started as an 8 team league has blossomed into an organization that now hosts 30 teams and over 250 players.

Combined Elements

Text Block

CSS selector:

p

Sample code:

<p>The Official Dodgeball Rules listed below have been constantly refined over the past 9 years to produce a style of dodgeball that is fast-paced, exciting, and fun. Feel free to use these dodgeball rules in your dodgeball activities. The Dodgeball Rules have a Creative Commons license, thus, please attribute the work properly. For the purpose of simplifying the language of the rules, all players, both male and female, will be represented with masculine pronouns.</p>

<p>Photo week is on February 3rd, so make sure your teams are all geared up with jerseys! Feel free to bring props and whatever else you need for your photos.</p>

Rendered element:

The Official Dodgeball Rules listed below have been constantly refined over the past 9 years to produce a style of dodgeball that is fast-paced, exciting, and fun. Feel free to use these dodgeball rules in your dodgeball activities. The Dodgeball Rules have a Creative Commons license, thus, please attribute the work properly. For the purpose of simplifying the language of the rules, all players, both male and female, will be represented with masculine pronouns.

Photo week is on February 3rd, so make sure your teams are all geared up with jerseys! Feel free to bring props and whatever else you need for your photos.
