About

The Richmond Dodgeball League was founded in the Fall of 2010 as an extension of the Vancouver Dodgeball League. What started as an 8 team league has blossomed into an organization that now hosts 30 teams and over 250 players.

In accordance with the International Dodgeball Association (IDA), the RDL operates under its Official Dodgeball Rules and is a proud member of its initiative to build a strong, social community around the game of dodgeball in the Richmond area.
