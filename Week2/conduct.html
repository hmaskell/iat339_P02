Rules Code of Conduct League Policy Headshot Policy

Spirit is of utmost importance at RDL and unsportsmanlike conduct, spirit breaches, and actions detrimental to the league will not be tolerated. This includes:

  1. Headshots and regular high throws (see new Headshot Policy)
    1. Regular hits above the shoulders (headhunting)
    2. Contested vs Uncontested head shots
  2. Contested headshots do occur, and are considered accidental (to a degree) or reasonable due to the play:
    1. The player is kneeling or crouching low
    2. The ball bounces off of something (ball/wall/ground/player) and hits a player in the head
    3. A person is throwing at another player and the recipient walks in front of the throw
    4. The person lowers their head into the ball
  3. Uncontested headshots are unacceptable and unsafe
    1. They occur in full standing position or similar (a slight crouch);
    2. Are uncalled for
    3. Directly contact the recipient without hitting anything else
    4. May also occur after a thrower has been consistently throwing high (despite being told otherwise by referees)
